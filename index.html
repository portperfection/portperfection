"I didn't grow up learning about Port. I grew up living inside it, and now I'm sharing everything it's given me."
Elias · Creator

Elias was born in September, during harvest, and has spent his entire life since in the Douro Valley. He grew up doing the physical work of the vineyards before becoming an enotourism guide at a historic Port wine quinta, greeting visitors from all over the world.

Port Perfection is his first audiobook: a family story that happens to be about wine, told through the people who shaped it, above all his mother, Maria, whose tireless work in the vineyards runs through every chapter.
