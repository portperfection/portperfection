"I didn't grow up learning about Port. I grew up living inside it, and now I'm sharing everything it's given me."
Elias · Creator

Elias grew up in the middle of the vineyards of the Douro Valley, doing the physical work as a child, long before he understood what any of it meant. Later, guiding visitors through the same quintas, he found himself living between two worlds: the valley he'd grown up inside, and the people arriving to understand it for the first time.

That's the story, and the lessons, Port Perfection sets out to share. By the final chapter, Port wine stops feeling complicated or intimidating. It becomes simple to enjoy, and all the more valued for finally being understood.
